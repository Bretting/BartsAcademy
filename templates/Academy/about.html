ABOUT BART'S ACADEMY

Welcome to class at Bart's Academy! The place where you can learn everything about our favorite topics: spirits and cocktails. It's the only school where doing fractions adds up to a delicious drink.

Bart's Academy is an online educational platform for cocktail professionals and at-home spirits enthusiasts. Learn all there is to know about spirits, cocktails, and brands through video classes, cocktail recipes by international bartenders, blogs, and brand deep dives. Whether you're greener than a splash of absinth or are a true bar veteran, there's always something to learn within our curriculum.

At Bart's Academy, we cover all the basics. What styles of rum are there? What is distilling? What is the process of fermentation? But as we are cocktail nerds at heart, we love to take you on a deep dive and geek out together just as much. To do so, we work with experts in all related fields. Bart's Academy is created as a collaborative learning platform, partnering with some of the best within the industry to create educational videos and blogs. (Sounds like you? Send us a message.)

FROM NECESSITY TO PASSION PROJECT

How it all started We'd rather not think back to 2020. But it's where it all started. During one of the many lockdowns, we sought a way to continue doing what we do best: sharing knowledge with people who love cocktails and spirits. And, like many others, we found our educational space online, hosting digital tastings and Q&As, distillery tours, and interviews with professionals in the field. Four years later, our little lockdown project has transformed into a full educational platform made in collaboration with cocktail and spirit icons.

ALL BOTTLES, NOT JUST BART'S

Bart;s Academy is made by the team of Cane & Grain and Bart;s Bottles - hence the name. But we don't pick favorites. We include all kinds of spirits from all sorts of makers and distributors. Would you like to have your brand represented in the academy? Reach out!
